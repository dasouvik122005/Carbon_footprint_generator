    About Our Methodology

    This calculator provides an *estimated* carbon footprint. The actual footprint can vary based on many factors not simulated here (e.g., specific manufacturing processes, vehicle load factors, real-time traffic, renewable energy use in supply chain).

    Simplified Data Sources:

    • Product Emissions: Based on general industry averages for manufacturing and raw material extraction.
    • Electricity Usage: Estimates CO2 from typical electricity consumption of electronics over their lifespan, using India's average grid emission factor.
    • Packaging: Estimated based on common materials (cardboard, plastic) and their production emissions.
    • Transportation: Uses average emission factors for different modes (road, rail, air) per tonne-kilometer, combined with simplified inter-state distances within India. Last-mile delivery is approximated, with options for different vehicle types.
    • Returns: Assumed to have a carbon cost similar to forward logistics, as products often travel back to warehouses.
    • End-of-Life (EOL): Simplified estimation of emissions from product disposal, considering recycling or landfill.

    Our goal is to raise awareness and encourage conscious online shopping, not to provide an exact scientific measurement. Data points are indicative and simplified for the project scope.
